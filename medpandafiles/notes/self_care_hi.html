Self-Care Activities

Engage in the following self-care activities:

1. Hand Hygiene

  • Frequent handwashing with soap or hand sanitizer Video - Hand Washing

2. Infection Control

3. Environmental Hygiene

  • Keep the room clean, well-ventilated, and dust-free Video
  • Disinfect frequently touched surfaces:
    • Phones Video
    • Doorknobs

4. Food Safety

Video - Food Safety
  • Avoid raw or undercooked foods (meat, eggs, sushi)
  • Rinse fruits and vegetables well before peeling
  • Cut away damaged or bruised areas before use
  • Keep hands, counters, dishes, cutting boards, sinks, and utensils clean
  • Wash dishtowels often
  • Use pasteurized milk and juices
  • Boil or sanitize sponges regularly
  • Avoid street food and food from unknown sources

5. Protective Measures

  • Avoid crowds and sick people
  • Wear face masks in public or hospital settings
  • Use gloves when handling contaminated items or bodily fluids
  • Visitors should follow hand hygiene
